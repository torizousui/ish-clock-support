ish clock

Time, the ish way.

About

ish clock shows you the time the way you actually talk about it. Not 7:04. Just 7-ish.

Time is displayed in 20-minute intervals — close enough to know where you are, vague enough to breathe.

FAQ

How does the time display work?

ish clock shows time in three patterns based on the minute:

  • 0–19 minutes → "7-ish"
  • 20–39 minutes → "half past 7-ish"
  • 40–59 minutes → "8-ish"

What is ish nudge?

ish nudge is a gentle alarm that rings within a 14-minute window around your chosen time. Set it for "7-ish" and it will nudge you somewhere between 6:53 and 7:07.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open ish clock, swipe right to open the Pro screen, and tap "Restore purchase".

How do I add the widget?

Long press your home screen or lock screen, tap the "+" button, and search for "ish clock".

Does ish clock work with Apple Watch?

Yes. Open the ish clock app on your Apple Watch to see the time, the ish way. You can also add a complication to your watch face.
